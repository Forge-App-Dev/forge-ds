Daily targets summary — kcal + 3 macros, with an "Ajustar" edit link.

```jsx
<TargetsCard kcal={2400} protein={150} carb={260} fat={70} accent="#10B981" onEdit={openTargetsForm} />
```

Distinct from `MacroMeter`/`MetaBar` (which show *progress toward* a target) — this shows the targets themselves as a flat summary.
